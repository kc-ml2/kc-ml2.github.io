We usually consider an environment with a Markov Decision Process(MDP) in Reinforcement Learning. Markov Decision Process $\langle\mathcal{X}, \mathcal{A}, r, P, \gamma\rangle$ is a Markov reward process with decisions, where $\mathcal{X}$ is the state space, $\mathcal{A}$ is the action space, $r$ is the reward function, $P$ is the transition function, and $\gamma \in$ (0,1) is the discount factor.
